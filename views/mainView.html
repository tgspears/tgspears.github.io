Rex

Daysie Chain

Daysie Chain is a service that allows you to easily and quickly organize your social life. With Daysie Chain, you can add your friends to groups, create events, send invites that automatically push RSVP-able notifications, and track attendances.

Developed with Alex Nguyen and Dave Raskin
